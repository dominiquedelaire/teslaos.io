TeslaOs.io is a free environment for Tesla cars that runs on the web browser of your Tesla (Model 3, Y, S, X, Cybertruck). It requires a Tesla internet connection to operate. It can also be run on a tablet or your PC / Mac.

TeslaOS.io offers a portal of interesting web applications in different areas: car charging, routes, games, weather, videos on demand, TV on demand, Tesla services, etc... We also create components of existing apps like Waze for coupled with the GPS position of the car. We regularly add new apps (games, video services, music, etc.) and new categories.
